Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BookWorm

Is BookWorm free to use?

Yes — BookWorm is completely free. There is no subscription plan, no premium tier, and no hidden fees. Every feature is available to all users at no cost.

Where is my reading and movie data stored?

All your data is stored locally in your browser on your own device. Nothing is ever uploaded to a server. This makes BookWorm one of the most private book trackers available — your reading life belongs only to you.

Can I track both books and movies with BookWorm?

Absolutely. BookWorm includes a dedicated book tracker and a separate movie tracker, both with ratings, reviews, wishlists, and genre filtering — all in one unified reading and watching diary.

Can I export my book or movie collection?

Yes. You can export your full book or movie collection as an Excel file at any time, and import existing spreadsheets to migrate your data into BookWorm — perfect for readers switching from another tracking app.
